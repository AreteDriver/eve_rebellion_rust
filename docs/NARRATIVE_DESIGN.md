# EVE Rebellion: Narrative Design Document

## The Elder Fleet Liberation Campaign

### Setting: YC110

The year is YC110 (23346 AD). For centuries, the Minmatar people have suffered under Amarr enslavement. Millions remain in chains across the Amarr Empire. But the time for waiting is over.

The **Elder Fleet** - a massive armada secretly constructed by the ancient Tribal Elders who had hidden in the Great Wildlands for generations - emerges from the shadows. Their mission: the largest liberation operation in New Eden's history.

You are a young Minmatar pilot, recruited into the Elder Fleet to fly liberation raids deep into Amarr space. From a rusty Rifter to a legendary Jaguar, you will rise through the chaos of war to become a hero of your people.

---

## Player Role

**Identity:** Young Minmatar pilot, recently recruited to the Elder Fleet
**Starting Ship:** Rifter-class frigate ("In Rust We Trust")
**Mentor:** Tribal Elder - voice of Fleet command, ancient wisdom
**Goal:** Free as many enslaved Minmatar as possible while dismantling Amarr defenses

### Character Progression
- **Rookie → Veteran → Ace → Legend**
- Ship upgrades reflect growing status in the Fleet
- Liberation count determines reputation and Elder recognition

---

## Narrative Arc (3 Acts)

### Act 1: "The Call" (Stages 1-4)
**Ship:** Rifter-class Frigate
**Theme:** Proving yourself, earning trust

The Elder Fleet has emerged, and chaos sweeps across the warzone. You're fresh meat - a rookie in a rusty Rifter assigned to convoy raids. Veterans doubt you. The Amarr underestimate you.

**Missions:**
1. Intercept lightly-defended slave convoy
2. Disable patrol escorts
3. Raid orbital supply depot
4. Assault minor Holder's estate station

**Tone:** Desperation meets hope. "In Rust We Trust!" becomes your mantra.

**Key Moments:**
- First liberation (visceral, emotional)
- Veteran pilot acknowledges your skill
- Elder takes notice: "Perhaps the young ones still carry the fire."

**Act 1 Climax:** Holder's Escort Fleet - your first real boss fight against organized Amarr resistance.

---

### Act 2: "The Storm" (Stages 5-9)
**Ship:** Wolf-class Assault Frigate
**Theme:** Full invasion, earning respect

The gloves are off. The Elder Fleet launches its full assault on Amarr infrastructure. You've proven yourself and earned a Wolf - no more rust, just steel. Imperial Navy responds in force.

**Missions:**
5. Assault Imperial Customs checkpoint
6. Destroy Inquisitor's enforcement vessel
7. Break through Navy Harbinger strike group
8. Disable stargate defense network
9. Core assault on Amarr Battlestation

**Tone:** Relentless momentum. "No more rust - just steel!"

**Key Moments:**
- Mass liberation from major holding facility
- Facing religious Inquisitor's fanaticism
- Elder: "You carry the fury of generations. Let it burn."

**Act 2 Climax:** Amarr Battlestation Core - cracking open a major slave processing facility.

---

### Act 3: "Liberation" (Stages 10-13)
**Ship:** Jaguar-class Assault Frigate
**Theme:** Historic victory, legend status

This is it. The final push into the heart of Amarr naval power. Carriers, dreadnoughts, and finally - an Avatar-class Titan stands between you and total victory. Centuries of slavery end today.

**Missions:**
10. Engage Imperial Navy Armageddon battleship
11. Assault Amarr Carrier and its fighter wings
12. Duel the Lord Admiral's Apocalypse flagship
13. Final Stand against Avatar-class Titan defense

**Tone:** Epic, legendary. "From rust to legend!"

**Key Moments:**
- Facing capital ships alone
- Elder's final blessing before Titan battle
- Liberation count reaches climactic milestone

**Act 3 Climax:** Avatar-class Titan - the Empire's ultimate response. Destroy it, and the liberation is complete.

## The Tribal Elder (Mentor Character)

A single, universal mentor voice representing the ancient Elders who orchestrated the Fleet. Gender-neutral, ancient, wise, fierce.

### Speaking Style
- Measured, deliberate
- References ancestors and history
- Never panics, even in crisis
- Occasionally shows glimpses of centuries of pain

### Sample Dialogue

**Mission Briefings:**

*Act 1:*
> "The Elders have watched from the shadows for centuries. Now we strike. Prove you are worthy to fly with the Fleet."

*Act 2:*
> "The Empire reels. Press the attack. Every slave freed is a soul returned to our people."

*Act 3:*
> "This is the hour our ancestors dreamed of. The chains break today."

**Post-Mission:**
> "Well flown, pilot. The Elders take note."
> "Another blow struck for freedom. Continue."
> "Your ancestors would be proud this day."
> "Steel and fire. That is what you have become."
> "The Empire bleeds. Do not let them recover."

**Liberation Milestones:**
- 100: "A hundred souls returned to freedom. The Elders see your worth."
- 500: "Five hundred chains broken. You carry the spirit of the Rebellion."
- 1000: "A thousand lives reclaimed. Songs will be sung of this day."
- 5000: "Five thousand. You have liberated a city. History will remember."
- 10000: "Ten thousand souls freed by your hand. You are legend now."

## Enemy Forces: The Amarr Empire

### Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Gold, bronze, white, cream
- **Weapons:** Beam lasers (golden/yellow projectiles)
- **Aesthetic:** Ornate, religious, oppressive grandeur
- **Contrast:** Clean gold vs. Minmatar rust/industrial

### Enemy Types

| Type | Amarr Unit | Behavior | Visual |
|------|-----------|----------|--------|
| Basic | Punisher | Linear approach, steady fire | Gold hull, simple |
| Kamikaze | Zealot Fanatic | Suicide charge, glowing | Burning engines |
| Weaver | Imperial Slicer | Fast sine-wave, harass | Golden trails |
| Sniper | Oracle Gunship | Hang back, long-range | Long beam weapons |
| Spawner | Bestower Transport | Deploy fighters | Large, slow |
| Tank | Maller Cruiser | Heavy armor, absorb | Thick plating |

### Boss Roster (13 Total)

**Act 1 (4 Bosses):**
1. **Slave Transport Overseer** - Bestower convoy leader with escort
2. **Patrol Commander** - Navy Omen with support frigates
3. **Station Defense Battery** - Orbital weapons platform
4. **Holder's Escort Fleet** - Minor noble's personal guard

**Act 2 (5 Bosses):**
5. **Imperial Customs Commandant** - Prophecy battlecruiser
6. **Inquisitor Vessel** - Religious enforcer with fanatical crew
7. **Navy Harbinger Strike Group** - Elite battlecruiser squadron
8. **Stargate Defense Grid** - Network of turrets and drones
9. **Amarr Battlestation Core** - Massive station with multiple phases

**Act 3 (4 Bosses):**
10. **Imperial Navy Armageddon** - Battleship with drone support
11. **Amarr Carrier** - Launches continuous fighter waves
12. **Lord Admiral's Apocalypse** - Flagship, heavy weapons
13. **Avatar-class Titan** - Final boss, multiple phases, ultimate challenge

## Liberation Mechanics

### Core Loop
1. Destroy Amarr ships/structures
2. Liberation pods eject from destroyed targets
3. Collect pods to "liberate" souls
4. Counter tracks total freed

### UI Elements
- **During Gameplay:** "SOULS LIBERATED: X"
- **End of Mission:** "Liberation Report: XX souls freed this mission"
- **Campaign Total:** "Total Freed: X,XXX"
- **Milestone Popups:** Special celebration at thresholds

### Liberation Sources
- Slave transports (5-10 per ship)
- Station modules (20-50 per module)
- Boss defeats (100+ per boss)
- Hidden pods in debris (bonus)

## Audio/Visual Themes

### Minmatar (Player)
- Industrial sounds, mechanical
- Rust colors: brown, orange, grey
- Engines that sound like they might fail
- "Junker" aesthetic with hidden strength

### Amarr (Enemy)
- Clean, choral undertones
- Gold, white, cream colors
- Humming beam weapons
- Oppressive religious grandeur

### Emotional Beats
- **Liberation pods:** Soft chime, hopeful
- **Elder speaks:** Deep reverb, ancient
- **Boss entrance:** Ominous bass, gold glow
- **Victory:** Triumphant horns, liberation count surge
